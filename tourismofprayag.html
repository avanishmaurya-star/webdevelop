prayagraj,uttar pradesh, India, is a historic city renowned for its stunning monuments like the sangam and anand bhawan, amar saheed chandrashekahr azad park
Visitors can explore the city's rich history and culture by visiting the following attractions:

Sangam:

The confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, considered a sacred site for Hindus.

Anand Bhawan:

The former residence of the Nehru-Gandhi family, now a museum showcasing their life and legacy.

Amar Shaheed Chandrashekhar Azad Park:

A sprawling park dedicated to the freedom fighter Chandrashekhar Azad, with lush greenery and recreational facilities.
